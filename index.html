Message From the Parish Priest

Our parish embraces the vibrant communities of Bulimba, Balmoral, Hawthorne, and part of Morningside, nestled along the Brisbane River from Norman Creek to Taylor Street and extending to Thynne Road. While our surroundings are undeniably beautiful, it is the faithful Catholic community that truly defines who we are.

At the heart of our parish life is the celebration of the Eucharist, uniting us in Christ’s love and mission. Each Sunday, we gather as a family of faith for three Masses. The Eucharist is our foundation, but our parish life extends far beyond the altar. Together, we strive to live out Christ’s ministry through acts of service, compassion, and fellowship, making His presence known in our midst.

As you journey with us, may you find a place where your faith is nourished, your spirit uplifted, and your heart welcomed. We hope that through the life of Sts. Peter and Paul’s, you will grow in love for God and neighbour and discover the joy of belonging to a community rooted in faith and fellowship.

Fr. Kevin Smith P.P

Dean, East Deanery

In Infant Baptism, the Church receives children into the family of God, cleansing original sin and bestowing sanctifying grace as the foundation of their spiritual journey.

Holy Matrimony

In the Sacrament of Matrimony, the union of husband and wife is sanctified, reflecting the love of Christ for His Church and fostering a holy partnership for life and family.

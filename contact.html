Flocknote is the Parish of Saints Peter and Paul's official communication platform, designed to keep parishioners informed and engaged in the life of the parish. By registering, you will receive timely updates regarding Mass times, parish events, sacramental preparation, ministry opportunities, and other important announcements directly to your email or mobile device.

We encourage all parishioners, whether newly joined or long-standing members, to register for Flocknote to ensure you remain connected with the parish community.

To register, please visit https://bulimbacatholic.flocknote.com/register or contact the parish office for further assistance.

How to Register for Flocknote – Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Have Your Email Address Ready: Flocknote uses your email to securely register you, so make sure you have access to your inbox.
  • Go to the Parish Flocknote Registration Page: Visit this link on your device (phone, tablet, or computer).
  • Enter Your Email Address: You will be prompted to enter your email address. Flocknote will send you a secure 4-digit verification code.
  • Check Your Email and Enter the Code: Open your email inbox, find the message from Flocknote, and enter the 4-digit code when prompted. This confirms your email is valid and secure.
  • Complete Your Registration Details: Fill in your name and contact information. You can also add your family members—similar to completing a parish census form.
  • Select the Groups You’d Like to Join: Choose which parish groups, ministries, or newsletters you’d like to receive updates from.
  • You're All Set! Once registered, you'll start receiving parish news, reminders, and updates tailored to the groups you've selected.

If you need assistance, please contact the parish office.
